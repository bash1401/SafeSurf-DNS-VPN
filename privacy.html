Privacy Policy — SafeSurf

Last updated: August 15, 2025

BashTech ("we", "us", or "our") operates the SafeSurf application ("SafeSurf" or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use SafeSurf.

1. Summary

SafeSurf provides DNS-over-HTTPS (DoH) functionality to encrypt DNS queries. We focus on privacy and do not collect or store user browsing histories or DNS query logs.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not log or retain DNS queries or the websites you visit.
  • We do not collect IP addresses for the purpose of logging browsing activity.
  • We do not collect contents of network traffic.

3. Information We May Collect

To maintain and improve the App we may collect minimal, anonymized diagnostic and performance information only, such as crash reports and anonymous usage statistics. This helps us fix bugs and improve stability. We do not link these analytics to personal identities.

4. How We Use Data

  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Measure feature usage in an anonymized way

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics or crash reporting. These services may collect limited anonymized data under their own policies. We recommend reviewing their policies if desired.

6. Security

We follow industry-standard practices to protect data. DNS requests performed via DoH are encrypted between your device and the DoH resolver. While we strive to protect data, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

7. Children

SafeSurf is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do — please review periodically.
